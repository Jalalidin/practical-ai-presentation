Your AI User Manual

Core Technical Concepts Made Simple

DATA

Training material (billions of text examples, images, conversations)

Like feeding a student millions of books before an exam

MODEL

The trained AI brain (GPT-4, Claude, etc.) with billions of parameters

Like a graduate who studied everything and can answer questions

PROMPT

Your instructions to the AI (the questions or tasks you give it)

Like asking a consultant a specific, well-crafted question

Perfect Prompt Recipe

The 3-Part Formula for Better Results

ROLE

Tell AI who to be

"Act as a world-class copywriter"

CONTEXT

Provide background information

"My audience is new homeowners"

TASK

Specify exact requirements

"Write 3 email subject lines under 10 words"

VAGUE PROMPT

"Write me some marketing copy"

❌ No role, context, or specific requirements

SPECIFIC PROMPT

"Act as a world-class copywriter. My audience is new homeowners aged 25-35 who just bought their first house. Write 3 email subject lines under 10 words with a professional yet friendly tone that promotes our home maintenance app."

✅ Clear role + context + specific task
